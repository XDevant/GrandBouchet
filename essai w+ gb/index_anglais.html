WELCOME TO  CHATEAU DU GRAND BOUCHET

Charming Apartments, Bed and Breakfast, Gites in the heart of the  Loire Valley

Located in  the heart of the Loire Valley, the Chateau  du Grand  Bouchet, haven  of  peace  and  greenery,  halfway between the nearby cities of Tours and Villandry, will be a perfect home base for a Loire Valley visit :discover  the  Loire Chateaux , the gardens of  Touraine,  and the superb  Vouvray and Chinon wineyards. Activities include hiking, biking, ballooning, golfing, canoeing ...
